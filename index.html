Abstract

This research, part of the line of Communication and Language of the Master in Education , focuses on describing the ability of a set of methodological teaching and learning strategies to develop an understanding of inferential reading of ninth grade public school students.

Project arises from a participatory research-action approach with the aim of contributing to the improvement of pedagogical practice of teachers, subjects of intervention. We sought to analyze and describe the cases in which the variables of educational practice and teacher affective dimension can be associated positively with improved processes of understanding of the inferential reading.

Two intervention groups were identified and two tests are performed to establish the initial base and the final state of the ability inferential reading comprehension.
